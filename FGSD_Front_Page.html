About Our Institution

F.G Science Degree College offers admissions in both Intermediate (HSSC/F.Sc) and Bachelor (BS) programs. We provide quality education in various fields to help students achieve academic success and personal growth.

Students can choose from different study groups at the Intermediate level and a range of BS degree programs in science and other disciplines.

Why study in science degree?

A science degree provides you with:

  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Strong foundation for various career paths
  • Opportunities for research and innovation
  • Understanding of the natural world and technology
  • Preparation for advanced studies in specialized fields

Our science programs are designed to foster curiosity and develop analytical skills that are valuable in any profession.

What you can study?

We offer a wide range of programs:

  • Intermediate Programs: Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, Computer Science, Statics, Economics
  • Bachelor Programs: BS Physics and BS Computer Science
  • Skills: Freelancing, Graphic designing and Digital marketing

Each program is carefully designed with modern curricula and taught by experienced faculty members.

Admission Information

Admission Criteria

  • Must have passed SSC (Matriculation) or equivalent
  • Minimum required marks: 4550% (varies by program)
  • Offered Groups:
    • Pre-Medical
    • Pre-Engineering
    • ICS (Computer Science)
    • General Science
  • Admissions are based on merit

Documents Required

  • Original SSC result card + photocopies
  • Character certificate
  • Form-B or CNIC
  • Passport-size photos (2 copies)
  • Migration certificate (if from another board)
